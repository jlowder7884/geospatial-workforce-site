Land Surveying Program

A structured, workforce-focused program designed to introduce students to the profession of land surveying and prepare them for CST Level 1 concepts.

Program Overview

This program provides a comprehensive introduction to land surveying, combining foundational knowledge, practical concepts, and real-world context to help students understand both the field and the career pathway.

The curriculum is designed with CST Level 1 preparation in mind, giving students exposure to the terminology, concepts, and thinking required for success.

  • Introduction to surveying principles and terminology
  • Survey equipment and field procedures
  • Safety practices and job site awareness
  • Measurement concepts and basic calculations
  • Career pathways in surveying and geospatial technology

Status: Active (delivered through Canvas)

How Courses Are Delivered

The Geospatial Workforce Institute is currently using a hybrid approach that combines the reliability of existing learning systems with the long-term vision of a fully independent platform.

Current Model

Courses are presently delivered through Canvas, allowing for structured lessons, quizzes, and student progress tracking while the website serves as the main public-facing platform.

  • Reliable course delivery
  • Organized modules and assessments
  • Easy transition for current students and teachers

Future Direction

Over time, the institute will continue building toward a more independent platform that includes AI-supported learning, expanded course access, and a more customized student experience.

  • Integrated AI tutor support
  • More direct control over learning experience
  • Scalable model for schools and workforce partners

What Students Can Expect

These courses are built to do more than deliver information. They are designed to help students understand the field, connect learning to real careers, and build confidence in their next steps.

Career Relevance

Students see how the material connects directly to real professions and real-world work.

Practical Concepts

Lessons focus on usable knowledge, field awareness, and skills that matter beyond the classroom.

Structured Growth

Course pathways help students build from awareness to competence in a clear, guided way.

AI-Supported Learning

Students have access to an integrated AI tutor within the course environment, providing on-demand support, concept reinforcement, and guided assistance throughout the learning process.

What It Does

  • Answers course-related questions
  • Reinforces key concepts and terminology
  • Helps students work through problems step-by-step

Why It Matters

The AI tutor extends learning beyond the classroom, giving students support when they need it without increasing teacher workload. This creates a more scalable and effective learning experience.
