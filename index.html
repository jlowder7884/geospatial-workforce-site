About the Institute

Geospatial Workforce Institute exists to expand access to high-quality geospatial and surveying education. Our mission is to support the talent pipeline by helping students, educators, and workforce partners connect classroom learning to real-world opportunity.

Our Mission

To prepare learners for success in surveying, mapping, and geospatial careers through practical instruction, relevant curriculum, and strong workforce alignment.

Our Vision

To become a trusted hub for geospatial workforce development by helping schools and organizations build stronger, more direct pathways into high-demand technical careers.

What makes our approach different

We focus on practical implementation, not just theory. The goal is to make geospatial education clear, relevant, and useful from day one.

1

Real-World Relevance

Learners are introduced to the tools, terminology, procedures, and expectations used across the surveying and geospatial workforce.

2

Workforce Alignment

Programs are designed to support career exploration, technical readiness, and stronger alignment with industry needs and certification pathways.

3

Scalable Delivery

Our model can support individual classrooms, pilot campuses, school districts, and broader workforce partnerships as demand grows.
